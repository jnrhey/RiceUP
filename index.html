App Features

The inspiration of this mobile application by the group 2 of it 212 is the question where we can buy rice and how can we differentiate rice from other rice. This will help the users to choose the appropriate rice for them in a right price. So, they can upgrade their way to buy rice hassle free.

Category

It categorizes the Short, Medium, and Long grain rice.

Bookmarks

Marks your favorite rice. This depends on the user.

Mode of payments

Here is the different kinds of payment by E-Wallet or cash on delivery.

Security

We used Firebase by Google because it is more Secure, they automatically encrypt our Customer Credentials.

Feedback

Reviews of the customer to the rice they have purchased.

Description

Gives the specific description of the rice.
